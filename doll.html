The Living Doll

It looked up with eyes full of innocence. The kind of innocence you found in youth before it was ruthlessly stripped away from them. The temporary kind.

General Information

Age: ~300 years old

Gender: Null

Pronouns: Any

Doll is defective. Under its bang lays an amber-colored, cracked eye that portrays its state. If Doll ever got fixed, the act would wipe its personality and return it to its default state. Having been abandoned by its creator and last owner with no next owner, Doll has no purpose or mission. It spends its days with its significant other, traveling and relaxing. Should it be given the opportunity to have a new owner, it would take it.

Roleplay Information

Post Length: 2-3 paragraphs

Rating: SFW

Genre: Slice-Of-Life

Prompt Example

Your muse is a very old immortal. One day, they find a peculiar little creature and take it into their home. Living with this being makes them feel something they haven't in a while. Some kind of parental protectiveness. They see it as one of their own. And with this in mind, they finally do something they've been wanting to for a while: starting a new life.
